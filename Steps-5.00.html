Positive Insight

The strategy's success lies in attracting 13,000 online passengers with lower fares ($553.10 average), yielding $7 million in revenue, while drawing 7,000 offline passengers at higher fares ($1,190.00 average), generating $9 million.



Negative Insight

Even though customers book online 86% more than offline, the total revenue generated from online bookings is $7 million, which represents a 22.22% decrease compared to the $9 million total revenue generated from offline booking.
